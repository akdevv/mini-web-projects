The first Iron Man suit, the Mark-I was created from Jericho missile parts by Tony Stark and fellow captive Ho Yinsen while they were being held captive in a cave in Afghanistan.
The suit was designed as a pacemaker to keep shrapnel from reaching Stark's heart, and as in the movie, was used to escape his captors.

Mark I

First Appearance: Iron Man

Status: Destroyed in Afghanistan

Designed as a prototype, this suit was used to explore flight potential and had almost no weapons, before quickly giving way to the Mark III. It was temporarily confiscated by the U.S. Military and was converted into the War Machine Mark I before Tony got it back and reverted it to the Mark II.

Mark II

First Appearance: Iron Man

Status: Confiscated by Military

The Mark III fixed the freezing problem by changing the suit to a gold-titanium alloy. It also added wrist-mounted missiles, hip-mounted flare launchers and shoulder-mounted machine guns. This was the first armor to feature the classic red and gold color scheme.

Mark III

First Appearance: Iron Man

Status: Destroyed by Iron Monger

After the Mark III took heavy damage in Tony's fight with the Iron Mongor, this suit was built as a direct replacement, with only a few minor changes from the previous model.

Mark IV

First Appearance: Iron Man 2

Status: Destroyed by the Mandarin

The Mark V was an emergency suit, designed to be portable by collapsing into a briefcase. This armor was destroyed during the attack on Stark's house in Iron Man 3.

Mark V

First Appearance: Iron Man 2

Status: Destroyed by the Mandarin

This armor had a new triangular shaped Unibeam on its chest piece. The armor also upgraded its artillery to include a grenade launcher in one arm, a missile launcher in a shoulder and metal-slicing super lasers in both arms (though this can only be used once). This armor was destroyed during the attack on Stark's house in Iron Man 3.

Mark VI

First Appearance: Iron Man 2

Status: Destroyed by the Mandarin

A fully loaded rapid deployment suit, it was built for heavy combat and was the first designed for automatic deployment and assembly. This armor was destroyed during the attack on Stark's house in Iron Man 3.

Mark VII

First Appearance: The Avengers

Status: Destroyed by the Mandarin

Known as the 'Iron Legion', the Mark VIII to Mark XLI suits were held in the Hall of Armors extension, located in the basement of Tony's Malibu mansion that was destroyed in Iron Man 3. As part of the 'House Party Protocol', he summoned them, controlled by his A.I. program J.A.R.V.I.S., to help defeat Aldrich Killian. After the battle, all the remaining suits were destroyed when Stark activated the 'Clean Slate Protocol'.

Mark VIII

First Appearance: Iron Man 3

Status: Destroyed by Clean Slate Protocol

Using biometric implants, Tony Stark could remotely connect with this armor, allowing him to become Iron Man from anywhere in the world.
